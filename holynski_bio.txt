Aleksander Holynski is a senior research scientist at Google DeepMind and a postdoctoral scholar at Berkeley AI Research, working with Alyosha Efros and Angjoo Kanazawa. He received a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Washington, where he was advised by Steve Seitz, Brian Curless, and Rick Szeliski, and a B.S. with Honors from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. His research focuses on computer vision, computer graphics, machine learning, and generative models. His work has been recognized with best paper awards at ICCV 2023 and CVPR 2024.
