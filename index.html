About me

I'm a research scientist at Google DeepMind and a postdoctoral scholar at Berkeley AI Research, working with Alyosha Efros and Angjoo Kanazawa. I did my PhD at the University of Washington, advised by Steve Seitz, Brian Curless, and Rick Szeliski. I received my B.S. at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I'm originally from San Juan, Puerto Rico.
